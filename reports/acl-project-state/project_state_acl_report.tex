\begin{abstract}
We summarize the current FairSpeech compression evaluation: 34 of 54 model--audio runs are validated, covering 900,014 utterance-level predictions from six ASR systems. Results so far show Qwen3-ASR leading in accuracy and compression robustness, but Black/African-American speakers remain the worst-WER group in every completed condition. Pending Canary and Granite runs can still change the Gen-3 comparison.
\end{abstract}

\paragraph{Status.}
This is an interim report. The strongest unresolved comparison is Gen-3 breadth: Canary-Qwen and Granite-Speech are still pending, and qwen3-asr-1.7b lacks MP3 32/16 metrics.

\paragraph{Next.}
Finish the remaining 20 runs, recompute WER/MMR tables, then rerank Gen-3 models by the joint objective: low WER, low absolute Black/AA gap, and stable degradation under 8 kHz and MP3 16 kbps.
