# Plan 02.1-02 Summary: ASHA + RsLoRA + Expanded HP Sweep

## What was delivered

Added ASHA pruning (HyperbandPruner) and RsLoRA rank-stable scaling to the packed sweep infrastructure. Ran two 100-trial sweeps (Stage 2: PLoRA + ASHA; Stage 3: PLoRA + ASHA + RsLoRA) and determined the final best config. Updated locked_config.json with the Stage 2 winner.

## Key results

- **Stage 2 (PLoRA + ASHA):** 100 trials in 358 min. Best: trial_077, rank=4, mlp=True, eval_loss=0.1424. Gate PASSED vs Stage 1 (2 shared configs).
- **Stage 3 (PLoRA + ASHA + RsLoRA):** 100 trials in 387 min. Best: trial_066, rank=8, mlp=True, cosine sched, warmup=0.1, eval_loss=0.1451. Gate PASSED vs Stage 2.
- **RsLoRA rank shift:** Optimal rank changed from 4 (Stage 2) to 8 (Stage 3). Top-10 rank distribution: Stage 2 = {4:8, 8:2}, Stage 3 = {8:7, 4:2, 32:1}.
- **Winner:** Stage 2 (no RsLoRA), eval_loss=0.1424. 10.5% improvement over Phase 2 sweep best (0.159).
- **Speedup:** 1.28x for Stage 2 (100 trials in 358 min vs 460 min sequential equivalent).
- **VRAM:** Stage 2 peak 12,153 MB, Stage 3 peak 10,293 MB (both under 13 GB budget).
